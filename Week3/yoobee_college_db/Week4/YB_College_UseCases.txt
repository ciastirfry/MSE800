Actors:

Student – Registers and enrolls in courses.
Lecturer – Assigned to courses.
Admin – Manages reports and oversees operations.
IT/DBA (under Admin) – Maintains and manages the database.
Auditor (under Admin) – Reviews reports and ensures compliance.


Use Cases:

Register Student: Student provides details for database entry.
Enroll in Course: Student selects and enrolls in available courses.
Assign Lecturer: Lecturer is assigned to courses by Admin.
Generate Reports: Admin and Auditor generate academic and operational reports.
Manage Database: IT/DBA maintains system data integrity and structure.
